Yercaud Lake, also known as Emerald Lake, is the centerpiece of Yercaud town. Visitors can enjoy boating, pedal boating, and rowing on the tranquil waters of the lake while soaking in the picturesque surroundings. The lake is surrounded by lush greenery and offers stunning views of the hills.
